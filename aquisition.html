ACQUISITION CRITERIA

The Acquisitions group under Zanahary is actively pursuing acquisition opportunities in selected geographic markets that have the appropriate mix of current income and long-term appreciation potential. Proven ability to lead our investment partners through the acquisition process capitalizing on our long history of investing and repositioning of and asset to maximize returns through:

Detailed and thorough underwriting analysis

Vetted and proven renovation and reposition plans.

Intensifying risk reward characteristics to maximize success.

Product Type: Multifamily - garden, mid-rise & high-rise

Product Sub-type: Value-add, core plus, redevelopment opportunities and infill development sites

Geographic Focus: Mid-Atlantic, Northeast and Southeast regions, and other areas opportunistically

Market Selection Criteria: Primary and secondary markets are selected opportunistically based on asset availability within geographic focus. Most favorable markets will feature strong population and employment growth characteristics. Transit-oriented and/or in path of growth.

Site Selection Criteria: Infill, high barrier-to-entry locations are preferred. Proximity to demand generators. For urban projects, proximity to mass transit is preferred. Strong visibility and convenient access to major thoroughfares.

Asset Size: 100 units - no maximum; large portfolios or large phased development projects preferred

Target Deal Size: Existing Properties - $5,000,000 minimum - no maximum; Land – dependent on land area
